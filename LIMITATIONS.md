
System Limitations
iOS Limitations
Cannot implement per-app audio routing for other apps
AirPlay only affects the app's own audio playback
No access to system-wide audio routing controls
Android Limitations
Per-app audio routing is OEM-specific
Samsung Separate App Sound is only available on Samsung devices with One UI
Non-Samsung devices must rely on Cast or bridge device
Limited access to system audio settings from apps
DRM and Service Compliance
No decryption or forwarding of DRM-protected content
All streaming must comply with service Terms of Service
Bridge presents as official Spotify Connect device via librespot
Hardware Limitations
Requires Raspberry Pi 3B+ or newer for reliable Bluetooth
Bluetooth range and quality may vary by speaker
AirPlay requires iOS 10+ and compatible devices
